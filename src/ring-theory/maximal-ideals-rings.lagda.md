## Idea

A {{#concept "maximal ideal" Disambiguation="in a ring"}} in a
[ring](ring-theory.rings.md) `R` is a proper ideal `I` of `R` such that for any
[ideal](ring-theory.ideals-rings.md) `J` containing `I` is either `I` or the
entire ring `R`.
